ZEANI-UNN COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT SERVICE EDUCATION (CDSE), LOCATED AT AFRICAN DEVELOPMENT FUND (ADF) BUILDING,FACULTY OF VOCATIONAL & TECHNICAL EDUCATION (VTE), UNIVERSITY OF NIGERIA NSUKKA

Open gateways in the form of scholarship and non-scholarship programme to assist Nigeria Vulnerable youths gain access to ordinary craft level and advanced craft level Education for gainful employment. Admission is now open for prospective Technicians, craft men and women to apply without discrimination of any kind.

Vision

To Produce competent and licensed Technicians, craft men and women that will take employment opportunities in the skill sector, provoke industrial revolution via various entrepreneurship activities and silence the negative effect of poverty, underemployment and unemployment ravaging the youths.

Mission

To be the best producer of competent and licensed Technicians, Craft men and women that will correct the negative effect of becoming an Engineer, Engineering Teacher, Technology & Vocational Education Teacher without passing through technician and craft level education and cadre respectively.
