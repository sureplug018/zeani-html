Admission

S/NO Qualification Title Entry Qualification Remark
1 National Technical Certificate (NTC)/ National Business Certificate (NBC) Entry qualifications is with or without pass at junior secondary school certificate examination, age is 16years and above The training contains 70% practice to 30% Theory, the duration is one year accompanied with three semesters for Experienced Candidates (EC), But for Campus Candidates (CC), it is three years with Nine semesters
2 Advanced National Technical Certificate (ANTC)/ Advanced National Business Certificate (ANBC) Entry qualifications is with or without pass at Senior secondary school certificate examination, age is 16years and above The training contains 70% practice to 30% Theory, the duration is one year accompanied with three semesters for Experienced Candidates (EC) and Campus Candidates (CC).
3 Modular Trade Certificate Entry qualifications is with or without pass at junior secondary school certificate examination, age is 16years and above The training contains 70% practice to 30% Theory, the duration is one year accompanied with three semesters for Experienced Candidates (EC) and Campus Candidates (CC).
3 WEAC Trade Entry qualifications is with or without pass at junior secondary school certificate examination, age is 16years and above The training contains 70% practice to 30% Theory, the duration is one year accompanied with three semesters for Experienced Candidates (EC) and three years, six semesters for Campus Candidates (CC).
3 NSQ Level 1-6 Entry qualifications: Recognition of prior knowledge via a practical test and ability to pass previous NSQ Levels. Academic qualifications are not a prerequisite, age is 16years and above The training contains 90% practice to 10% Theory, the duration is three months accompanied with three qualification exams for Experienced Candidates (EC) and one year, three semesters for Campus Candidates (CC).

Benefits

  • Opportunities to be harvested by the industries for potential employment through our annual job fair programmes with our accredited and approved qualifications from NBTE, Federal Ministry of Education (FME), Federal Executive Council (FEC) and National Council in Education (NCE)
  • Opportunity to connect with customers and investors, sell your brand and product, then gain opportunity to contest for award recognition through our annual trade fair programmes
  • Opportunity to participate in our one-time camped semester recreational programme within and outside the country during your training
  • Opportunity to gain admission into the University of Nigeria Nsukka Degree programmes at Direct Entry (DE) level (200level) or indirect entry level(100level) including any other university or higher institution of your choice.
  • Secondary Education is not needed on completion of our ordinary craft level education for job placement and continued academic progress to higher education in Nigeria
  • Middle level Education is not needed on completion of our Advanced craft level education for job placement and continued academic progress to higher education in Nigeria
  • Decent limited hostel accommodations are also available

    • Types of Candidacies

    Experience Candidates (EC): - Are those with high level of experience in their choice of trade and have also prepared themselves through various educational trainings to pass the examinations. Such candidates are free to register as Experience Candidates. Experience Candidates are not required to undergo the training hence only one-time semesters/examinations fee is required to be able write the examination.

    Campus Candidates (CC): - Are those that will undergo various educational trainings in their choice of trade, pass all the semester examinations, and pay all the semester fees. Such candidates are free to register as Campus Candidates.

    Target Audience

  • Person or group of persons with or without formal education but need technical training education to fit into a professional role
  • Vulnerable persons seeking to work with ordinary craft level and Advanced craft level qualifications
  • Tertiary Institution Graduates, Artisans and tradespersons willing to enhance their technical knowledge
  • JAMB candidates seeking direct entry admission in University of Nigeria Nsukka and other Nigeria Higher institutions.
    • Only Shortlisted candidates will be admitted into the programme
  • Women and those that could not gain admission within the disciplines covered by our programmes in the University of Nigeria Nsukka degree programme are encouraged to apply
  • Parents are also encouraged to enroll their children in our ordinary and advanced craft level education since the qualifications earned qualifies them for job placement and ranking in Nigeria civil service, industries and continued academic progress to higher degrees in Nigeria.
